## Invite Me To Speak

I enjoy speaking with engineering leaders, founders, architects, and product teams that are building the next generation of digital financial services.

If your audience is exploring practical transformation topics, I can deliver sessions that connect strategy with implementation.

## Signature Talks

### 1. The Architecture of Trust: Building Reliable Digital Banking Systems
*Keynote / Conference Session — 30–45 min*

Digital banking systems must be secure, scalable, and compliant — simultaneously. This session explores the architectural patterns that underpin high-availability banking platforms: event-driven design, layered security, and the failure modes that cause customer trust to collapse. Audiences leave with a practical framework for evaluating their own platform's resilience.

**Best for:** Engineering conferences, CTO forums, banking technology summits

---

### 2. Build vs Buy in Core Banking: A Decision Framework That Actually Works
*Workshop / Talk — 45–60 min*

The build-vs-buy question in financial services is rarely answered well. This session walks through a structured evaluation framework covering strategic importance, total cost of ownership, vendor lock-in risk, and compliance constraints — illustrated with real platform decisions. Includes a hands-on checklist exercise.

**Best for:** Product and engineering leadership, fintech founders, digital transformation teams

---

### 3. AI in Financial Services: What the Hype Gets Wrong
*Keynote / Panel — 30–45 min*

AI adoption in regulated domains follows different rules than consumer tech. This session examines where AI creates genuine leverage in banking (fraud, personalization, document processing) versus where it creates unacceptable risk — and how to build governance frameworks that let teams move fast without regulatory exposure.

**Best for:** Innovation teams, compliance-aware engineering leaders, digital banking product teams

---

### 4. Developer Experience as a Competitive Advantage in Regulated Domains
*Engineering Leadership Talk — 30–45 min*

In banks and fintechs, developer experience dies by a thousand compliance cuts: slow pipelines, fragmented tooling, and risk-averse change management. This session makes the business case for DX investment and shows practical patterns — feature flags, trunk-based delivery, and internal platform thinking — that improve velocity without compromising controls.

**Best for:** Engineering managers, platform teams, CTO-track leaders

---

### 5. Channel Banking Architecture: Delivering Consistency Across Every Customer Touchpoint
*Technical Deep-Dive — 45 min*

Mobile, internet, branch, agent — customers move across channels and expect a consistent, seamless experience. This session unpacks the architecture of a multi-channel banking platform: API gateway design, session and state management, and the integration patterns that prevent channel silos from fragmenting the customer experience.

**Best for:** Architects, senior engineers, platform leads in retail banking
