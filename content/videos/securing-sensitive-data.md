## Overview

Sensitive data protection is a design concern from the first architecture conversation. It cannot be bolted on after teams have already copied data across systems and logs.

## Key Ideas

- Classify data before designing storage, APIs, logs, analytics, and support workflows.
- Reduce exposure through tokenization, masking, encryption, retention limits, and least-privilege access.
- Observability must help support teams without leaking private customer information.

## Takeaway

Secure systems minimize where sensitive data travels, who can see it, and how long it remains accessible.
